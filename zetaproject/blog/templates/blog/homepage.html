    Welcome

    Welcome to my webpage! Whether you came here on purpose or by accident, all are welcome and encouraged to look around. If you came to learn more about my qualifications, then here is a great place to start. My name is, obviously, Chase Leffers and I am a self-proclaimed visionary, futurist, and student of life. I grew up on science fiction and fantasy books, and I quickly learned that my drive throughout life is to make the wonderful technologies in those stories possible and accessible. I believe that such a future is possible only with concerted effort-- Technology does not magically imporove. It only improves when a group of peopel decide to dedicate their life to make something new and to push the envelope of human achievement. I might just be a student right now, and I might be applying to an entry-level job(or maybe I've already begun to make my own path and you're looking to be hired by me), but my dreams go far beyond the monotonous or menial. If any of what you've read so far has struck a cord with you, then you're the kind of person I want to keep close, the kind of person I would love to work with to create a new world.
